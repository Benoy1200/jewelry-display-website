Custom vs Standard Display Cases

Last Updated: December 2025 10 min read By JewelryDisplayPro Team

Choosing the right display case is a strategic decision that directly impacts product presentation, security, and overall cost. Below we break down the two main options – custom‑made cases and off‑the‑shelf (standard) cases – and help you decide which fits your business model.

What You'll Learn

  • Design flexibility differences between custom and standard cases
  • Cost structure and budget considerations
  • Delivery timelines for each option
  • Best use cases for custom vs standard solutions
  • Key decision factors for your business

1. Design Flexibility

  • Custom Cases: Tailored dimensions, glass thickness, lighting integration, branding embossing, and unique internal compartments. Ideal for flagship stores or high-value collections that require a distinctive visual identity.
  • Standard Cases: Fixed sizes and configurations, limited branding options. Suitable for rapid rollout across multiple retail locations.

2. Cost Structure

因素 定制展柜 标准展柜
单价 (USD) $1,200 – $3,500 $400 – $900
模具/工程费 $500 – $1,200 (一次性) 无 无
批量折扣 10% – 15% (≥50件) 20% – 30% (≥100件)

3. 交付周期

  • Custom: 4–8 weeks (设计 → 样品 → 量产)
  • Standard: 1–2 weeks (库存现货)

4. 适用场景

  1. 高端珠宝旗舰店、博物馆展柜 – 需要独特造型与灯光方案 → 推荐定制
  2. 连锁珠宝零售、快时尚饰品店 – 需要快速部署、成本控制 → 推荐标准
  3. 临时展会或活动场地 – 需求弹性大 → 可选标准或小规模定制

5. 关键决策要点

在决定之前，请评估以下因素：

  • 品牌定位：是否需要独特的视觉符号？
  • 预算与 ROI：定制的额外费用能否通过提升销量或品牌诊价收回？
  • 时间窗口：项目启动前是否有足够的设计与生产周期？
  • 供应链能力：是否已有可靠的定制供应商？

结论

没有绝对的答案：最佳方案往往是混合模式——核心旗舰店使用定制展柜，其他门店采用标准展柜，以实现品牌统一与成本平衡。
