2. Handles

Handles contribute significantly to the overall aesthetic of your display case:

  • Surface Finishes: Polished, brushed, or matte finishes are available.
  • Material Options: Stainless steel (corrosion-resistant) or brass (premium appearance).

3. Brackets & Rails

Brackets and rail systems provide structural support for shelving and sliding mechanisms:

  • Load Capacity: Each bracket typically supports up to 50 kg.
  • Rail Types: Linear, dual-track, or concealed systems.
