How to Choose Hardware for Display Cases

Last Updated: December 2025 8 min read By JewelryDisplayPro Team

Choosing the right hardware ensures durability, safety, and a premium look. Below we compare the most common components used in professional jewelry display cases.

What You'll Learn

  • Key differences between hinge types and their load capacities
  • Handle materials and their impact on aesthetics
  • Bracket and rail systems for different display needs
  • Cost comparison and recommended brands

1. Hinges

Hinges are critical for door functionality and long-term reliability. Key considerations include:

  • Materials: Stainless steel, brass, and aluminum alloy are the most common options.
  • Load Capacity: Standard hinges support 5–30 kg; heavy-duty models can handle up to 80 kg.
  • Recommended Brands: Häfele, Hettich, Sugatsune.

2. Handles

Handles contribute significantly to the overall aesthetic of your display case:

  • Surface Finishes: Polished, brushed, or matte finishes are available.
  • Material Options: Stainless steel (corrosion-resistant) or brass (premium appearance).

3. Brackets & Rails

Brackets and rail systems provide structural support for shelving and sliding mechanisms:

  • Load Capacity: Each bracket typically supports up to 50 kg.
  • Rail Types: Linear, dual-track, or concealed systems.
