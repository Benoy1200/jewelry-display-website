How to Choose Hardware for Display Cases

Last Updated: December 2025 8 min read By JewelryDisplayPro Team

Choosing the right hardware ensures durability, safety, and a premium look. Below we compare the most common components used in professional jewelry display cases.

What You'll Learn

  • Key differences between hinge types and their load capacities
  • Handle materials and their impact on aesthetics
  • Bracket and rail systems for different display needs
  • Cost comparison and recommended brands

1. 铰链 (Hinges)

  • 材质：不锈钢、黄铜、铝合金。
  • 负载：常规 5 kg – 30 kg，重型可达 80 kg。
  • 推荐品牌：Häfele、Hettich、Sugatsune。

2. 把手 (Handles)

  • 表面处理：抛光、拉丝、哑光。
  • 材质选择：不锈钢（耐腐蚀）、黄铜（高端感）。

3. 支撑杆 & 滑轨 (Brackets & Rails)

  • 承重设计：每根支撑杆最大 50 kg。
  • 滑轨类型：直线、双轨、隐藏式。
